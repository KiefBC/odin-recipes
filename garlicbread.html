Garlic Bread

Description:

Garlic bread is a popular side dish that pairs well with pasta, salads, and many other main courses. Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, this bread is slathered with a delectable garlic butter mixture that's sure to delight any garlic lover.

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of Italian or French bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for topping)

Directions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Garlic Butter Mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Slice the Bread: Cut the loaf of bread in half horizontally. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  4. Bake: Place the bread halves on a baking sheet, cut side up, and bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden and crispy.
  5. Optional Broil: For a more golden top, you can broil the bread for an additional 1-2 minutes, but keep a close eye on it to prevent burning.
  6. Serve: Remove from the oven and, if desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Slice and serve warm.

Note: For a spicy kick, you can add some red pepper flakes to the garlic butter mixture. If you prefer softer garlic bread, wrap the buttered bread in aluminum foil before baking.
